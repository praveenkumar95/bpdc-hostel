The BITS Pilani Dubai Campus Hostel, located in Dubai International Academic City, was established in the year 2008.

 

The hostel provides seperate housing facilities for both boys and girls. The entire hostel is divided into 5 blocks. For the boys namely A,B,C and D block (categorized year wise) and G block for all the girls. Each hostel is provided with a residential warden and other essential staff.

 

The hostel rooms are fully air-conditioned and furnished.  Apart from the rooms, facilities such as gymnasium, laundromat and mess are also provided to the residents.
