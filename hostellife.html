Hostel Life

Hostel Life

Apart from the rooms, various other facilities are available seperately for both boys and girls. The hostel offers mess food for the residents. There are seperate gyms, one common for all four blocks for boys and one for the girls block.  And laundromats that function 24//7.

 

Each hostel block has a common room where students can play indoor games, watch television, chat and relax.
