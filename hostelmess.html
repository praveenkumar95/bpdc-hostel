The hostel offers mess facility for all the residing students; seperately for both boys and girls. All varities of indian dishes are served catering to the tastes and recommendations of students. It serves regular meals and breakfast with its own set of timings apart from snacks and other food items that are available from morning to night. The mess serves both veg and non-veg food. Although only veg is served during lunch except fridays. Snacks are served in the evenings along with tea/coffee. The mess has this tradition of a food festival once in a while on special occasions.

 

Apart from these there is a cafeteria, which is accesible for both hostellers and day scholars. The cafeteria functions from morning to evening and again later in the night from 9 pm till midnight, exclusively for hostellers and for those day schoars who stay back. There is also a vending machine and a coffee machine just outside the messes in both the girls and boys block.
