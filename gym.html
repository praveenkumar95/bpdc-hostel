The hostel offers seperate gymnasium facilities for both boys and girls. For the boys, it is located in the C Block and for the girls, in G Block. The timings are made suitable such that residents can work out either during early morning or evening depending upon their choice. All kinds of equipments, ranging from most common to the most unique ones are available. 

 

The gym is extremely clean and well-maintained. The authorities have employed regular cleaners who come on a daily basis for keeping the gym clean.
