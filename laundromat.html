The hostel provides a 24/7 laundromat service for its residents. The laundromat is equipped with automatic washing machines, clothes dryers and large ironing boards for ironing and folding clothes. The users will have to purchase laundry tokens seperately for both washing machines and dryers. The tokens can be bought from the respective block wardens.
